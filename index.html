Himachal 5 Nights / 6 Days

Himachal: Shimla & Manali Tour

A breathtaking hill journey through colonial charm, alpine valleys, snow-dotted adventures, and scenic highways from Chandigarh arrival to Ambala departure.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Chandigarh Arrival & Drive to Shimla

Arrive at the designated Chandigarh Airport / Railway Station pickup point. Meet your private Pixlo Holidays representative and board the AC vehicle for a scenic drive uphill to Shimla. Check into your hotel.

Day 2: Shimla & Kufri Sightseeing

Enjoy breakfast and head out to explore Kufri, Green Valley, and Fagu Valley. Spend your evening walking along The Ridge, Mall Road, Christ Church, and Scandal Point. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the Shimla hotel.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali via Kullu Valley

After breakfast, check out and drive towards Manali. En route, catch vistas of the Pandoh Dam and stop by the vibrant Kullu river rafting points and local shawl weaving factories. Check into your Manali hotel for dinner and rest.

Day 4: Manali Local Exploration & Jogini Waterfall Trek

Embark on an adventurous trek to Jogini Waterfall alongside a local guide. Visit the historic Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Hot Springs, Tibetan Monastery, and Van Vihar. Evening free to stroll around Manali Mall Road.

Day 5: Solang Valley & Atal Tunnel Excursion

Drive to Solang Valley for snow sports and paragliding activities. Cross through the iconic Atal Tunnel (subject to weather and permit availability). Return to the hotel for a special complimentary candle-light dinner.

Day 6: Manali to Ambala Departure

Have breakfast, check out from the hotel, and board your return transfer vehicle heading towards Ambala Railway Station / Airport for your onward journey home.

✓ Inclusions

  • ✓ Complete inter-city transfers and sightseeing via dedicated AC vehicle.
  • ✓ 1 Night in Shimla and 3 Nights in Manali.
  • ✓ 5 Breakfasts and 5 Dinners at hotels.
  • ✓ Local trekking guide for Jogini Waterfall.
  • ✓ Tolls, parking, driver allowances, and state transport taxes.
  • ✓ Complimentary candle-light dinner and flower bed decoration.

✕ Exclusions

  • ✕ 5% GST additional on the final invoice.
  • ✕ Rohtang Pass entry charges are extra and self-borne.
  • ✕ Adventure sport equipment fees, snow suit rentals, and pony rides.
  • ✕ Room heater deployment charges and laundry tips.
  • ✕ Additional costs due to landslides, roadblocks, or weather anomalies.

Goa 3 Nights / 4 Days

Go Goa: North & South Goa Tour

A vibrant coastal escape blending sunlit beaches, heritage sites, scenic viewpoints, and relaxed evenings by the sea.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Goa Arrival & Leisure

Get picked up from Goa Airport or Madgaon/Thivim Railway Station by a Pixlo Holidays chauffeur. Check into your beachside hotel. Spend the day adjusting to the Goan vibe or visiting the local markets. Dinner at hotel.

Day 2: North Goa Beach & Fort Tour

After breakfast, drive out for North Goa sightseeing. Visit the 17th-century Fort Aguada, followed by the vibrant Vagator Beach, Anjuna Beach viewpoint, and the lively shacks of Baga Beach and Calangute Beach. Return to hotel by evening.

Day 3: South Goa Heritage & Cruise Experience

Explore the historic side of Goa. Tour the UNESCO heritage site Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral, and Mangeshi Temple. Head to the Mandovi River cruise point for a scenic sunset river cruise.

Day 4: Departure from Goa

Enjoy a final Goan breakfast, check out, and receive a timely drop-off at the airport or station for your journey home.

✓ Inclusions

  • ✓ Airport / Railway Station pickup and drop-off via private car.
  • ✓ 3 Nights stay at a standard boutique property.
  • ✓ 3 Breakfasts and 3 Dinners.
  • ✓ 1 Full Day North Goa and 1 Full Day South Goa tour.
  • ✓ Access to the hotel swimming pool where operational.
  • ✓ Parking fees, fuel costs, and local state driver toll taxes.

✕ Exclusions

  • ✕ 5% GST extra on total invoicing.
  • ✕ River cruise tickets, Snow Park entry, and Fort Aguada monument fees.

Kerala 4 Nights / 5 Days

Magical Kerala Tour

A serene southern journey from Cochin to Munnar, Thekkady, and Alleppey, unfolding tea gardens, wildlife, culture, and backwaters.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Cochin Arrival to Munnar Hill Station

Arrive at Cochin Airport/Station. Meet your driver and drive towards Munnar. On the way, stop by Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Check into your Munnar resort.

Day 2: Munnar Tea Gardens Exploration

Spend a full day exploring Munnar. Visit Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, the lush green Rose Garden, and the Eravikulam National Park to spot the Nilgiri Tahr. Return for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary

Drive from Munnar to Thekkady. Check into your hotel and head out to see spice plantations. In the evening, enjoy traditional Kathakali and Martial Arts (Kalaripayattu) live shows.

Day 4: Thekkady to Alleppey Houseboat Experience

Proceed to Alleppey (Alappuzha). Board a traditional Kerala Houseboat for a cruise across the tranquil backwaters. Enjoy an authentic regional lunch and dinner served right on board.

Day 5: Alleppey to Cochin Departure

Disembark from the houseboat after breakfast. Return transfer to Cochin for your evening train or flight back home.

✓ Inclusions

  • ✓ 2 Nights in Munnar, 1 Night in Thekkady, and 1 Night in Alleppey.
  • ✓ 4 Breakfasts and 4 Dinners at the properties.
  • ✓ Entry tickets to the Kathakali and Martial Arts cultural shows.
  • ✓ Complete inter-city transfers and sightseeing via dedicated AC vehicle.
  • ✓ Driver allowances, state border taxes, fuel, and parking.

✕ Exclusions

  • ✕ 5% statutory government tax extra.
  • ✕ Airfare or train tickets from your originating city.
  • ✕ Eravikulam National Park safari tickets and Periyar boating slips.
  • ✕ Premium or luxury upper-deck cruise modifications.
