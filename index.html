Himachal 5 Nights / 6 Days

Himachal: Shimla & Manali Tour

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Chandigarh Arrival & Drive to Shimla
  • • Arrive at the designated Chandigarh Airport / Railway Station pickup point.
  • • Meet your private Pixlo Holidays representative and board the AC vehicle.
  • • Scenic drive uphill to Shimla with breathtaking mountain views.
  • • Check into your hotel and unwind for the evening.
Day 2: Shimla & Kufri Sightseeing
  • • Enjoy breakfast and head out to explore Kufri and Green Valley.
  • • Visit Fagu Valley for scenic viewpoints and local experiences.
  • • Evening walk along The Ridge, Mall Road, Christ Church, and Scandal Point.
  • • Dinner and overnight stay at the Shimla hotel.
Day 3: Shimla to Manali via Kullu Valley
  • • After breakfast, check out and drive towards Manali.
  • • En route, catch vistas of the Pandoh Dam.
  • • Stop by the vibrant Kullu river rafting points and local shawl weaving factories.
  • • Check into your Manali hotel for dinner and rest.
Day 4: Manali Local Exploration & Jogini Waterfall Trek
  • • Embark on an adventurous trek to Jogini Waterfall with a local guide.
  • • Visit the historic Hadimba Devi Temple and Vashisht Hot Springs.
  • • Explore the Tibetan Monastery and Van Vihar.
  • • Evening free to stroll around Manali Mall Road.
Day 5: Solang Valley & Atal Tunnel Excursion
  • • Drive to Solang Valley for snow sports and paragliding activities.
  • • Cross through the iconic Atal Tunnel (subject to weather and permit availability).
  • • Return to the hotel for a special complimentary candle-light dinner.
Day 6: Manali to Ambala Departure
  • • Have breakfast and check out from the hotel.
  • • Board your return transfer vehicle heading towards Ambala.
  • • Drop-off at Ambala Railway Station / Airport for your onward journey home.

  • ✓ Complete inter-city transfers and sightseeing via dedicated AC vehicle.

  • ✓ 5 Breakfasts and 5 Dinners at hotels.

  • ✓ Tolls, parking, driver allowances, and state transport taxes.
  • ✓ Complimentary candle-light dinner and flower bed decoration.

  • ✕ 5% GST additional on the final invoice.

  • ✕ Adventure sport equipment fees, snow suit rentals, and pony rides.
  • ✕ Room heater deployment charges and laundry tips.
  • ✕ Additional costs due to landslides, roadblocks, or weather anomalies.

Thailand 4 Nights / 5 Days

Enchanting Thailand: Pattaya & Bangkok Tour

A captivating 5-day journey from Bangkok to Pattaya featuring coastal relaxation, island adventures, and cultural exploration of Thailand's iconic temples and gems.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Private Transfer to Pattaya
  • • Arrive at Bangkok Airport and meet the representation team.
  • • Board a comfortable private vehicle transfer from the airport directly to your hotel in coastal Pattaya.
  • • Check into your Pattaya accommodation and take time to unpack, settle in, and refresh.
  • • Spend your first evening at complete leisure relaxing at your hotel or exploring nearby streets.
  • • Experience an overnight stay in Pattaya to rest after your international journey.
Day 2: Coral Island Tour with Lunch
  • • Enjoy a freshly prepared, complimentary morning breakfast served right at your hotel.
  • • Set out on the scenic Coral Island Tour utilizing a shared Seat-In-Coach (SIC) transit system.
  • • Spend your day swimming in clear coastal waters, walking on sandy beaches, or trying optional beach sports.
  • • Refuel during your island excursion with an authentic, complimentary local lunch.
  • • Head back to the mainland for another relaxing overnight stay in Pattaya.
Day 3: Free Leisure Day in Pattaya
  • • Start your morning with a filling hotel breakfast to power up for the day.
  • • Take advantage of a completely free day for self-exploration to set your own holiday pace.
  • • Head out to explore the world-famous nightlife and street food stalls of Pattaya Walking Street.
  • • Relax along the tropical shoreline or swim in the warm waves at the popular Jomtien Beach.
  • • Return to your hotel room at the end of the evening for your final night's stay in Pattaya.
Day 4: Temple Sightseeing & Transfer to Bangkok
  • • Fill up on your daily morning hotel breakfast before checking out of your room.
  • • Board a comfortable private transfer vehicle to journey from coastal Pattaya to Bangkok.
  • • Stop en route to marvel at the massive, 5.5-ton solid gold idol at Wat Traimit (Golden Buddha).
  • • Explore the sprawling grounds of Wat Pho, famously known as the largest temple compound in Bangkok.
  • • Tour the world-renowned Gems Gallery to view spectacular, authentic Thai jewelry and gemstones.
  • • Check into your designated hotel city room for an exciting overnight stay in Bangkok.
Day 5: Hotel Checkout & Airport Departure
  • • Enjoy your final traditional hotel breakfast in the capital city before checking out.
  • • Wrap up any last-minute packing or store your bags safely at the front desk.
  • • Board your arranged ground transportation to head back to the main international airport.
  • • Coordinate your departure timing directly with your flight schedule to ensure a stress-free check-in.
  • • Conclude your 5-day holiday tour with wonderful, lasting memories of Thailand.

  • ✓ 4 Nights accommodation (3 nights in Pattaya, 1 night in Bangkok).
  • ✓ Daily breakfast served at the hotels (except on day of arrival).
  • ✓ Complimentary lunch during the Coral Island excursion.
  • ✓ Private vehicle transfers from Bangkok Airport to Pattaya, Pattaya to Bangkok, and Bangkok to Airport.
  • ✓ Seat-In-Coach (SIC) transfers for the designated sightseeing days.
  • ✓ Guided city tour inclusions for Wat Pho, Wat Traimit, and Gems Gallery.
  • ✓ All local toll taxes, driver allowances, and standard agency service charges.

Philippines 4 Nights / 5 Days

Cebu Island Adventure: Heritage & Marine Tour

An exciting 5-day journey through Cebu featuring Spanish heritage sites, world-famous sardine runs, canyoning adventures, and pristine island hopping.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cebu & Heritage Exploration
  • • Arrive at Mactan-Cebu International Airport and meet your ground transfer team.
  • • Take a private vehicle transfer to your hotel in central Cebu City to check-in and refresh.
  • • Step back in time by touring the historic Spanish military structure of Fort San Pedro.
  • • View Magellan's Cross and the adjacent Basilica Minore del Santo Niño, the country's oldest church.
  • • Try iconic local culinary specialties like crispy Cebu Lechon (roast pig) for a welcome dinner.
  • • Spend an overnight stay at your city hotel to recharge for the journey ahead.
Day 2: Moalboal Sardine Run & Sea Turtles
  • • Enjoy a complimentary breakfast served early at your hotel before checking out.
  • • Board a private south-bound vehicle transfer to the coastal town of Moalboal (approx. 3 hours).
  • • Put on snorkeling gear at Panagsama Beach to witness the world-famous massive Sardine Run.
  • • Swim alongside wild sea turtles grazing on the shallow reefs just a few meters off the shore.
  • • Check into your beachside Moalboal resort and spend the evening watching a quiet coastal sunset.
Day 3: Kawasan Falls Canyoning Adventure
  • • Fuel up with a traditional hotel breakfast to prepare for an active day.
  • • Travel to nearby Badian for an adrenaline-pumping canyoning adventure through jungle cliffs.
  • • Jump off limestone ledges, slide down natural rock waterslides, and swim through turquoise river canyons.
  • • Emerge at the majestic multi-tier Kawasan Falls to relax and swim in the Gatorade-blue pools.
  • • Eat a complimentary local lunch served at the foot of the waterfalls before returning to Moalboal.
Day 4: Marine Sanctuary Island Hopping
  • • Start your day with a hot hotel breakfast before heading to the boat pier.
  • • Board a traditional motorized outrigger boat for a private marine sanctuary excursion.
  • • Snorkel the vibrant, coral-rich marine walls of Pescador Island to spot colourful reef fish.
  • • Sail back to the mainland for a free afternoon to lounge on the white sands of White Beach (Basdaku).
  • • Check out of your resort and take an evening private transfer back to a resort on Mactan Island.
Day 5: Mactan Island Exploration & Departure
  • • Have your final tropical breakfast at the resort before handling checkout formalities.
  • • Visit the historic Lapu-Lapu Shrine to see the bronze statue honoring the native Philippine hero.
  • • Buy traditional souvenirs, local dried mangoes, and handmade acoustic guitars at a local market town.
  • • Board your pre-arranged private airport transfer to head back to the international terminal.
  • • Check in for your departure flight home with unforgettable memories of the Philippine islands.

✓ Inclusions

  • ✓ 4 Nights accommodation (2 nights in Cebu City/Mactan, 2 nights in Moalboal).
  • ✓ Daily breakfast served at your designated hotels (excluding arrival day).
  • ✓ Complimentary lunch provided on the Kawasan Falls/Canyoning tour day.
  • ✓ Private vehicle transfers from Cebu Airport to Hotel, Cebu to Moalboal, and Moalboal to Airport.
  • ✓ All boat rentals and outrigger fees for the private snorkeling and island-hopping excursions.
  • ✓ Local tour guides, safety gear (life jackets, helmets), and environmental fees for canyoning.
  • ✓ All local toll taxes, driver allowances, and standard agency coordination fees.

✕ Exclusions

  • ✕ International or domestic airfare tickets to and from Cebu.
  • ✕ Philippine tourist visa fees or electronic travel registration charges.
  • ✕ Daily lunches and dinners (unless explicitly listed as complimentary on tour days).
  • ✕ Snorkeling gear rental (masks, fins, and snorkels can be rented locally for a small fee).
  • ✕ Personal expenses such as laundry, telephone calls, tipping, and hotel mini-bar items.
  • ✕ Entrance tickets or fees to monuments and activities not mentioned in the day-by-day itinerary.
  • ✕ Mandatory comprehensive travel insurance for foreign entry.

Malaysia 4 Nights / 5 Days

Marvelous Malaysia: 5-Day Kuala Lumpur & Highlands Tour

A captivating 5-day journey through Malaysia featuring Kuala Lumpur's iconic landmarks, Putrajaya's administrative center, Batu Caves, and Genting Highlands with cable car rides.

Day-by-day itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Putrajaya Transit
  • • Meet and greet at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA).
  • • Transfer to your hotel via Putrajaya, Malaysia's administrative centre known as the "Intelligent Garden City".
  • • Check-in and evening at leisure.
Day 2: Batu Caves & Genting Highlands
  • • Morning departure to Batu Caves for a 30-minute photo stop at the iconic rainbow stairs and Murugan statue.
  • • Proceed to the Genting Highlands resort.
  • • Take the Awana SkyWay Cable Car over the rainforest canopy, with an optional stop at Chin Swee Cave Temple.
  • • Return to Kuala Lumpur for an overnight stay.
Day 3: Half-Day Kuala Lumpur City Tour
  • • Sightseeing across the city's key historic and futuristic landmarks.
  • • Photo Stops: Petronas Twin Towers, King's Palace (Istana Negara), National Monument, National Mosque, and Independence Square.
  • • Drive-by & Culture: Drive past the Botanical Garden and Chinatown.
  • • Shopping: Visits to the Cocoa Boutique chocolate factory and the Jadi Batek Gallery.
Day 4: Self-Exploration / Leisure Day
  • • A full free day dedicated to self-exploration, local shopping in Bukit Bintang, or booking optional day excursions.
Day 5: Departure
  • • Morning hotel check-out after breakfast.
  • • Seat-in-coach (SIC) transfer back to KLIA for your onward flight home.

✓ Inclusions

  • ✓ Arrival Assistance: Dedicated meet-and-greet assistance by representatives at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA).
  • ✓ Accommodation: 4 nights of hotel accommodation (typically 3-star, 4-star, or luxury tiers based on your chosen pricing matrix) with standard check-in at 3:00 PM.
  • ✓ Meals: Daily breakfast at the hotel, alongside specific meals explicitly outlined day-by-day in the itinerary.
  • ✓ Transit & Sightseeing: Private or Seat-In-Coach (SIC) transfers for the full-day Genting Highlands tour and the half-day Kuala Lumpur city tour.
  • ✓ Genting Cable Car: 2-way return tickets for the Awana SkyWay Cable Car to Genting Highlands.
  • ✓ Mandatory Local Taxes: The Government of Malaysia Tourism Tax of 10 RM per room, per night is standardly pre-calculated/covered within basic logistics fees.

✕ Exclusions

  • ✕ Flights & Rail: International flight tickets, domestic flights (unless booking the Langkawi variant), train fares, and airport visa costs.
  • ✕ Theme Park Tickets: Entrance passes for the Genting Highlands indoor or outdoor theme parks (Skytropolis/SkyWorlds), or optional full-day trips to Sunway Lagoon.
  • ✕ Monument & Museum Entry Fees: General admission fees to scale inside specific landmarks, parks, museums, or the Petronas Twin Towers observation deck.
  • ✕ Personal Incidentals: Daily laundry services, tipping for drivers/guides, soft drinks, alcoholic beverages, and bottled water outside regular meals.
